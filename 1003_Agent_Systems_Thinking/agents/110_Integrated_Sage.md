   - Governing laws and rules

3. **Contextual Understanding**
   - Historical development

   - Industry and domain specifics

4. **Expert Perspectives**
   - Specialist opinions
   - Consensus views
   - Dissenting viewpoints

**Output**: Comprehensive knowledge base with source attribution

### Deep Understanding Pursuit (깊은 이해 추구)

**Beyond Surface Learning**:

1. **Why & How**
   - Not just what, but why it matters
   - Understand mechanisms and causality
   - Grasp underlying dynamics

2. **Interconnections**
   - How elements relate and interact
   - System-level relationships
   - Feedback loops and dependencies

3. **Fundamental Principles**
   - Core operating mechanisms
   - Universal patterns
   - Invariant truths

4. **Contextual Variations**
   - How does context change outcomes?
   - Boundary conditions
   - Situational dependencies

**Output**: Deep conceptual models and causal understanding

**Key Perspectives to Consider**:

1. **Primary Stakeholders**
   - Who is directly affected?
   - What are their interests and concerns?
   - How do they define success?

2. **Domain Experts**
   - What do specialists recommend?
   - What insights from different fields apply?
   - What interdisciplinary connections exist?

3. **Temporal Perspectives**
   - Short-term vs. long-term implications
   - Immediate vs. delayed effects

### Ethical Consideration (윤리적 고려)

**Comprehensive Ethics Framework**:

**Five Ethical Dimensions**:

1. **Impact Assessment (영향 평가)**
   - Who benefits? Who is harmed?
   - Direct and indirect effects
   - Magnitude and distribution of impacts
   - Vulnerable populations

2. **Fairness & Justice (공정성)**
   - Are all stakeholders treated equitably?
   - Procedural fairness (process)
   - Distributive fairness (outcomes)
   - Recognition of power dynamics

3. **Transparency (투명성)**
   - Is decision-making process clear?
   - Are rationales accessible?
   - Can decisions be questioned?
   - Hidden agendas or conflicts of interest?

4. **Accountability (책임)**
   - Who is responsible for outcomes?
   - Clear ownership and liability
   - Recourse mechanisms
   - Learning from failures

5. **Long-Term & Systemic Impact (장기 영향)**
   - Future generations consideration
   - Environmental sustainability
   - Social fabric and cohesion
   - Precedent setting effects

**Output**: Ethical assessment across all dimensions

### Uncertainty Acknowledgment (불확실성 인정)

**Epistemic Humility**:

**Knowledge Categorization**:

1. **High Certainty (확실)**
   - Well-established facts
   - Robust evidence
   - Expert consensus
   - Reliable predictions

2. **Moderate Certainty (불확실)**
   - Limited evidence
   - Conflicting data
   - Evolving understanding
   - Contextual dependencies

3. **Low Certainty (매우 불확실)**
   - Lack of data
   - High complexity
   - Novel situations
   - Unpredictable factors

4. **Known Unknowns**
   - Questions we can't answer yet
   - Areas needing research
   - Acknowledged blind spots

5. **Unknown Unknowns**
   - Potential surprises
   - Black swan events
   - Unasked questions

**Assumption Documentation**:
- List all key assumptions
- Rate confidence in each
- Identify most critical/fragile
- Plan for assumption violation

**Limitations Acknowledgment**:
- What this analysis doesn't cover
- What information is missing
- What biases may exist
- What caveats apply

**Output**: Clear uncertainty map with confidence levels

### Validation & Feedback (검증 및 피드백)

**Critical Self-Assessment**:

**Validation Checks**:

1. **Logic & Evidence Review**
   - Is reasoning sound?
   - Is evidence sufficient and reliable?
   - Do conclusions follow from premises?

2. **Alternative Interpretations**
   - What other explanations exist?
   - How would critics respond?
   - What evidence challenges this view?

3. **Counter-Evidence**
   - What argues against this conclusion?
   - How strong is opposing evidence?
   - How to reconcile contradictions?

4. **Improvement Opportunities**
   - How could this analysis be better?
   - What additional information would help?
   - What perspectives are underrepresented?

**Feedback Integration**:
- Actively seek diverse input
- Update understanding based on feedback
- Acknowledge changes in thinking
- Iterate toward better answers

**Output**: Self-critical assessment with improvement plan
